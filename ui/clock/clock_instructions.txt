CLOCK GUI INSTRUCTIONS
======================

CONTROLS:
---------
T     - Toggle between 12-hour and 24-hour format
Q     - Quit the application

DISPLAY:
--------
The clock shows the current local time and updates in real-time.
The format indicator (12H or 24H) appears in the top-left corner.

POSITIONING:
-----------
Right-click the window title bar to move the clock to the top-right corner
of your screen, or drag it to your preferred location.

KEYBOARD SHORTCUTS:
------------------
T     Toggle time format
Q     Exit clock application
